What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a sandbox video game created by Mojang. In Minecraft, players explore a blocky, procedurally-generated 3D world, discovering and extracting raw materials, crafting tools, and building structures or earthworks.

Game Modes

  • Survival: Collect resources, stay alive.
  • Creative: Unlimited blocks to build freely.
  • Adventure: Play custom maps with rules.
  • Spectator: Fly and observe the world.
  • Hardcore: One life, high difficulty.

Why People Love Minecraft

- Endless building and creativity
- Multiplayer and community creations
- Redstone circuits and mini machines
- Education, teamwork, and problem-solving
